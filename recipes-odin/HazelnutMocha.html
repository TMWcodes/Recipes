Hazelnut Mocha au Lait

WHAT YOU'LL NEED

  • 40ml (2Tbsp) Hazelnut Syrup
  • 230ml (8oz) Mocha Flavored Coffee
  • 115ml (4 oz) whole milk
  • 70ml (1/4 cup) whipped cream
  • 19ml (1 Tbsp) chocolate syrup
  • 6ml (1 tsp)chocolate (for garnish)

METHOD

  1. Pour Hazelnut Syrup into a mug and add brewed Mocha Flavored Coffee. Stir.
  2. Froth milk and pour over the coffee
  3. Top with whipped cream and drizzle with chocolate syrup.
